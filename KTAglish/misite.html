The Great Patriotic War

World War II for the USSR began due to a sudden attack by Germany. the enemy made its way into the interior of the country to Moscow, which is why it was called the Great Patriotic War. But the fearless Soviet soldiers withstood and repulsed the enemy, taking Berlin and raising the Soviet flag over it. The entire war for the USSR lasted 4 years
